High-frequency posting warning

You're scheduling posts more often than every 12 hours. This pattern can look unnatural for smaller accounts.
  • Small / new accounts: ~1 post every 24–48h looks like healthy growth.
  • Mid-size accounts: ~1 post every 6–12h can be OK if quality stays high.
  • News / large accounts: 1 post every 1–2h is normal.

If you're not a high-volume news account, consider slowing down a bit so it feels organic and sustainable.
